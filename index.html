Wenfei Yu

Clinical Research Assistant • OSU Wexner Medical Center, Department of Psychiatry

I currently work as a Clinical Research Coordinator in the Mindfulness Lab at The Ohio State University Wexner Medical Center, where I coordinate clinical study operations and support multimodal data collection and analysis across behavioral, EMA, physiological (HRV), and neuroimaging (EEG, fMRI) measures.
My research examines how early-life experiences, sociocultural context, and emotion regulatory processes contribute to the development of youth psychopathology and variability in treatment response.
I am committed to improving the efficacy and accessibility of psychological interventions, particularly for underserved communities.
